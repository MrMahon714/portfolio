The Player

Built for the Game

Anthony Mahon is a 19-year-old versatile footballer with competitive experience spanning MLS Next, USL League Two, and international youth football. Born in Colombia and raised in Orlando, Florida, he has pursued this dream since the age of five — earning every level through relentless work ethic and a tactical intelligence rarely found at his age.

As versatile player, Anthony brings genuine pace and directness from either flank, the defensive intensity to function as a wingback or center back, and the ability to contribute as a forward or deeper midfielder. He accumulated 12 goals and 6 assists in his senior high school season and 13 goals in his Orlando City Premier campaign.

Dual US and Colombian citizenship. Fully eligible in CONMEBOL and CONCACAF. Actively seeking a professional contract or trial opportunity with a 2nd or 3rd division club in Europe.

Beyond the Pitch

Anthony Mahon is a Computer Science student at the University of Central Florida (UCF), pursuing an accelerated B.S./M.S. pathway expected to conclude in December 2026. His GPA of 3.74 follows a Magna Cum Laude record at Polk State College. He graduated high school ranked 18th out of 165 with 100% Bright Futures scholarship coverage.

At UCF, Anthony performs strongly to support the rigorous and math-heavy daily curriculum — work demanding the same pattern recognition and disciplined iteration that define his game. He has been recognised as an Outstanding Engineering Student, received College Board African American Recognition, and scored 5/5 on AP Spanish, among other honors
